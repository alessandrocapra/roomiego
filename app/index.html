We are here to help You

Introducing Roomiego: a flatmate matching and room finding service. We want to make looking for a place to live less stressful and less time consuming by saving you time having to set up countless flat viewings. We are here to help you find flatmates you will get along with.

Sounds good? Scroll down to find out how it works!

First, you match on 3 key measures

Need for privacy

Do you want a social flat or would you prefer some alone time? Live with someone who feels the same way .

Cleanliness

Do you want your house to be picture perfect or are you a bit more relaxed about that?

Reviews

See reviews and comments from previous flatmates to verify they are who they say they are.

Explore their profile

Explore their social media profiles, get a sense of who they are, what are their interests and hobbies. Perhaps you both are Game of Thrones fans and when living together will watch the latest episodes over dinner ?

Get to chat with them, meet them in real life!

Upload videos to introduce each other and see the flat videos and photos. Have a live video meeting if you want.
